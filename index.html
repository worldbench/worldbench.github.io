About WorldBench

WorldBench is an open research initiative focused on building, evaluating, and understanding generative world models for embodied AI and autonomous systems.

Our work spans full-spectrum benchmarking of driving world models, large-scale surveys of 3D/4D modeling, vision-language-action architectures, and open-source evaluation toolkits.

We believe that rigorous evaluation and open collaboration are essential for advancing world models from visual realism toward genuine physical understanding.
